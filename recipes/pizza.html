Pizza Recipe

Description

This classic Margherita pizza recipe uses fresh San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and sweet basil. To perfectly preserve the aromatic oils of the basil, the leaves are lightly added after the pizza comes out of the oven.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pizza dough, room temperature
  • 1 (14-oz) can whole San Marzano tomatoes (blended until smooth)
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, torn or sliced
  • 1 bunch fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 500F(260c). If you are using a pizza stone or baking steel, place it in the oven to preheat for at least 45 minutes.
  2. Shape the Dough: Stretch your pizza dough into your desired thickness on a floured surface or a cornmeal-dusted pizza peel.
  3. Top the Pizza: Spread the blended tomato juice/sauce leaving a 1-inch border around the edge. Evenly distribute the fresh mozzarella pieces over the sauce. Lightly salt and drizzle with olive oil.
  4. Bake: Bake for 10 to 12 minutes (or 6 to 8 minutes on a hot preheated pizza stone) until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  5. Serve with Basil: Remove the pizza from the oven and immediately top with fresh basil leaves. The residual heat will soften the basil and release its aromatic flavor without burning the leaves. Slice and serve.
